Sources

Primary dataset

Across the Timeline — WNBA player transactions, attendance, schedules and standings, drafts and draft lotteries, awards and award votes, All-Star selections, GM surveys, and NCAA Division I AP rankings and tournament data. Created by Kurtis Zimmerman and the Across the Timeline team.

Chart and table data

2025 WNBA MVP voting (chart and full ballot table): Across the Timeline, award votes. Indiana Fever season records, league and team attendance figures, 2024 Rookie of the Year voting, NCAA career scoring totals, No. 1 pick career lengths, and league team counts: league reports, NCAA records, and Across the Timeline; these figures are preliminary and will be verified against our cleaned dataset before final publication. Maximum salary figures come from published collective bargaining agreement reports (secondary sources), as salary data is not included in our core dataset. Scoring ranks on the Greatness page are computed from the same verified 2025 season data used on the Awards page. The attendance map was built by our team in Tableau from Across the Timeline's yearly attendance-by-team data (2025 season for active franchises; each folded or relocated franchise's final season), transcribed from the original site and hosted on Tableau Public.
