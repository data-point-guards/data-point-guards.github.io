Sources

Primary dataset

Across the Timeline — WNBA player transactions, attendance, schedules and standings, drafts and draft lotteries, awards and award votes, All-Star selections, GM surveys, and NCAA Division I AP rankings and tournament data. Created by Kurtis Zimmerman and the Across the Timeline team. Every figure, chart, and map on this site is drawn from this archive unless a caption says otherwise. Photographs come from Wikimedia Commons under Creative Commons licenses, credited in the caption beneath each image. The hand-drawn illustration that opens each chapter was created for this project.

Scholarship

Each chapter of this project is grounded in peer-reviewed research. Citations follow the Chicago Manual of Style, and every DOI below has been checked against the publisher’s record.
